Mexican Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Add corn and tuna into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add mayonnaise.
  3. Mix vigorously.
  4. Enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

1 serving:

  • 179 calories
  • 9.2g fat
  • 30mg cholesterol
  • 290mg sodium
  • 5g carbohydrate
  • 1g sugars
  • 2g fiber
  • 17g protein
