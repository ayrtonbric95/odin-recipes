Sweet and Salty Ramen

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of Maruchan Soy Sauce Flavor Ramen
  • 1/2 cup corn flakes
  • 1 cup butter toffee peanuts
  • 1/2 cup golden raisins
  • 1/2 cup coconut chips
  • 1/2 cup smoked almonds
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter puff cereal
  • Frying oil, as needed

Directions

  1. Cook 2 packages of Maruchan Soy Sauce Flavor Ramen noodles in 4 cups boiling water for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally, then turn off the heat. Add the contents of the seasoning packet and stir well for 1 minute until seasoning is fully dissolved.
  2. Strain off the broth, then let noodles cool and dry on a sheet tray.
  3. Heat frying oil to 400â„‰ and deep fry noodles for 2-3 minutes until super crispy. Strain off excess oil and add to a mixing bowl.
  4. Add corn flakes, toffee peanuts, golden raisins, coconut chips, smoked almonds, chocolate chips and peanut butter puff cereal, toss and enjoy!
